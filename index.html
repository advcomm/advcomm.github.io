Advanced Communications is a Pakistan based software house that specializes in working with international clients for their long term ‘software product development needs’.

With almost all customers, we engage in the incubation and ideation stage of their products and then continue to work with them post production and support.

For some customers we also run a 24x7 support center with subject matter experts sitting on support desk all the time.

We started in 1997 and continue to work with many of our long-term clients. Most company names are anonymized for their preference.

Key Milestones & Achievements

  1. 2020: Built a “Deep Package Inspection” gateway for a client in Canada. The packet would implement blacklists for a number of reasons. Both upstream and downstream data was inspected. The device was also available as a CPE running on small IoT Linux devices.
  2. 2014: Delivered a “Service Delivery Platform” for our Canadian client that handled ‘money transfer services’ for two GSM operators in Africa. The platform is still in operation delivering around 8 million transactions per day.
  3. 2011: Upgraded the Wi-Max solution to support 4G services including a PCRF and HSS solution in addition to end-to-end device provisioning solutions including CPEs.
  4. 2010: Designed a telecom billing solution for Wi-Max operators. The solution would provide real-time prepaid billing and would integrate with voice and data networks at the same time and manage balance in a single wallet. The solution was deployed in Wateen telecom (Abu Dhabi Group) and Wi-Tribe (Qatar Telecom subsidiary)
  5. 2008: A hosted VoIP service. Based on the AdvancedVoIP.com product. The service was live till 2009.
  6. 2006: Launched a basic web hosting service with the name HostAutomate.com
  7. 2005: Designed and delivered a lawful intercept solution. The solution accumulated CDRs from all the national telecom vendors and run detailed analytics with special consideration to LEAs.
  8. 2004: Design an Interconnect Billing Solution and a Converged Retail billing solution. The product was deployed in multiple international telecoms including Malaysia Telecom, Orascom Telecom, Softel (Germany) and others.
  9. 2003: BeyondPhone.com A VoIP Service that was offered with brand name of ‘AdCalls’. Featured in Wall Street Journal and other magazines, the service was one of the first apps worldwide that had an Ads based revenue model while using were making free calls. The service operated till 2005.
  10. 1999: Released the first set of ASP components under the name ASPFusion.com
  11. 1997: AdvancedVoIP.com Started to work on a VoIP billing system integrated with open source softswitch system. First version was released in 1999 and the product was used by more than 400 VoIP providers worldwide and continued to operate till 2009.
  12. 1997: Started work on a web hosting automation platform and delivered a ‘control panel’ in 1999. The product was used and is continued to be used by hundreds of data centers and web service providers.
